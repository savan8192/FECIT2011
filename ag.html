Author's Guidance


Extended Abstract Submission Guidelines

The aim of the conference is to provide a platform for the dissemination and exchange of technological knowledge on emerging areas of Electronics, Communication and Instrumentation Technology and their various applications in our daily life and earth sciences based industries. Abstract should clearly describe the purpose of study, principal results and major conclusions. The abstract (in .doc/.docx/.pdf format) must be limited to 500 words with the following formats

Title
Centered, Single Line Spacing, Times New Roman, 24, Bold Authors Name & Affiliation with full address for correspondence and e-mail: Centered, Single Line Spacing, Times New Roman, 11

Body of the Abstract
Justified, 1.5 line spacing, Times New Roman, 10

Address for correspondence
A soft copy of the extended abstract should be submitted through e-mail: fecit2011@gmail.com, as an attachment.

Deadline for abstract submission

August 30, 2011




Full Paper Submission Guidelines

On acceptance of abstracts, full paper (in .doc/.docx/.pdf ) limited to 4-5 pages will be asked to submit in the IEEE format

Address for correspondence
A soft copy of the full paper should be submitted through e-mail: fecit2011@gmail.com, as an attachment.

Deadline of submission for full paper

October 03, 2011
