Call For Papers

The intending authors are requested to submit extended abstracts (not exceeding 500 words) of their papers through e-mail:fecit2011@gmail.com on or before Aug. 30, 2011. The abstract should be in IEEE format containing the title of the paper, the name(s), of the author(s), their affiliation and full address for correspondence including telephone/fax numbers/E-mail.

All the papers should be submitted in the IEEE format
